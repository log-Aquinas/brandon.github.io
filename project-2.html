Colorama

An app developed for color-blind people

The essence of color-blind is the absence of cone cells in the human eye. The direct consequence is that it is difficult to distinguish light of a certain wavelength.If we use a certain algorithm to detect the indistinguishable colors in the picture, and replace or shift them to colors that are easily distinguished by color-blind patients, without destroying the beauty of the picture, we can optimize the vision of color-blind people to some extent experience.
